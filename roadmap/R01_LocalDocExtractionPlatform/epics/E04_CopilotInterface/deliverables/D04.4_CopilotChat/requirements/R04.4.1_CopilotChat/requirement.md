## Requirement Statement
Provide a chat interface for document-scoped and corpus-scoped questions with evidence citations.

## Acceptance Criteria

2. Responses include evidence pointers or citations.
3. Conversation history is preserved within the session.
4. Errors are handled gracefully with user-friendly messaging.
5. All interactions stay local (no cloud dependencies).
